SAP Transformation Navigator

How to help customers to plan their future SAP product map

SAP Transformation Navigator is a self service tool for all SAP customers and partner. It provides recommendations and guidance for their way into a digital future. In short words - you add the products you are currently running an the tool gives you recommendations on what is the optimal product and how it evolves over time.
